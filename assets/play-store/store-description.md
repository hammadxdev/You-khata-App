## Full Description

**Transform Your Business Management with Your Khata**

Your Khata is the ultimate business management app designed specifically for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and freelancers. Say goodbye to paper ledgers and complicated spreadsheets – manage your entire business from your smartphone!

### 🎯 **Key Features**

**📊 Customer Management**
- Add and organize unlimited customers
- Store contact information and transaction history
- Quick search and filter options
- Customer profile management

**💰 Transaction Tracking**
- Record sales, purchases, and payments instantly
- Track credit and debit transactions
- Automatic balance calculations
- Transaction history with detailed records

**📈 Reports & Analytics**
- Generate comprehensive business reports
- View profit/loss statements
- Track customer payment patterns
- Export reports for accounting

**🔒 Security & Privacy**
- Secure local data storage
- App lock with biometric authentication
- Data backup and restore options
- Privacy-focused design

**🎨 User Experience**
- Beautiful, intuitive interface
- Dark and light theme support
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Optimized for performance
